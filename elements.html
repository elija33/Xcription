Founded by Kenneth Amponsa who has a B.S in Exercise Science at The College of New Jersey in 2013. He carries CSCS USAW L1, CFNS, ACSM and Hall of Fame. He also received a certification as a Certified Strength and Condition Specialist through National Strength and Coaching Association, Olympic weightlifting and Certified Fitness and Nutrition Specialist through Exercise and Nutrition Works. Kenneth is a Strength Coach with over 10 years of Fitness and Nutrition experience. He specializes in strength, weight loss, nutrition, military, speed, agility and core training. One stops for individuals who are committed to make a lifestyle change long-term. Through the counseling in nutrition and fitness, your health will improve now and for years to come. Ken harvested his experience through research and working in a variety of areas including: University, Colleges, Hospital, Health Centers and Local Fitness Centers. Olympic weightlifting. Kenneth is a trainer who believes a healthy lifestyle choice and structured training will go a long way.
